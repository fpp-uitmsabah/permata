Professional Summary

Dr. Mohd Rozie Mohd Damit is a seasoned finance professional with over 17 years of comprehensive experience in credit management, banking operations, and financial services. Currently serving as Credit Manager at Perkasa Trading Sdn. Bhd. since 2019, he has successfully led credit divisions, reduced non-performing accounts, and implemented strategic credit policies. His academic credentials include a recently completed Doctor in Business Administration (DBA) from Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM), an MBA, and a Bachelor of International Finance from Universiti Malaysia Sabah. He is a certified credit professional with designations from AICB and IBBM, bringing both theoretical depth and practical expertise to finance and business management.
