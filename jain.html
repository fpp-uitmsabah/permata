Professional Summary

Dr. Jain Yassin is a leading environmental economist and educator in Malaysia, whose work centers on sustainable development, environmental pollution trends, and green economic transformation. He is a Senior Lecturer and researcher at the Faculty of Business and Management, Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM), Sabah Branch, with a distinguished academic profile centered on the critical intersection of economic development, environmental sustainability, and public policy.
