Professional Biography

Dr. Jasmine Vivienne Andrew is a dynamic force at the forefront of marketing academia, serving as a distinguished Senior Lecturer at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM). Her career is a testament to the power of translating rigorous scholarship into tangible business impact. Dr. Andrew's groundbreaking research dissects the complexities of service quality, pioneers studies into digital consumer behavior, and champions entrepreneurship within Malaysia.

"A dedicated educator and forward-thinking scholar committed to mentoring the next wave of business leaders and empowering a thriving, digitally-savvy Malaysian economy."
