Professional Summary

A distinguished economist and Associate Professor specializing in economic forecasting, applied econometrics, and artificial intelligence applications in economics. Notably became UiTM's youngest PhD graduate at age 25, and continues to contribute significantly to state-level policy papers and economic forecasting initiatives.
