Professional Summary

A dynamic and award-winning finance lecturer at UiTM Sabah with a passion for integrating technology into education. As a Google Certified Educator and one of Malaysia's first Quizizz Super Trainers, he excels in creating engaging, gamified learning experiences. His research focuses on sustainable finance, FinTech, and risk management, contributing actively to both academic literature and practical financial tools.
