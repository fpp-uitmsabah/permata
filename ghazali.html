Professional Summary

Ahmad Ghazali Bin Ismail is a dedicated Lecturer in Economics and Business Management at the Faculty of Business and Management, UiTM Sabah. His research is centered on applied economics with a regional focus on Malaysia and ASEAN. He specializes in **entrepreneurship's impact on economic performance**, **energy economics**, and **international trade**. Using quantitative methods like time-series analysis and econometric modeling, his work provides valuable, evidence-based insights for policymakers and contributes significantly to academic discourse on regional development.
